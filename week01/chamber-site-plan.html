Overview

Site Purpose

The Kadark Chamber of Commerce provides support to businesses to promote local economic activity. We are also working to revitalize the town by making use of its regional characteristics, and are working to preserve and pass on traditional events such as various festivals.

Scenarios

  • Support for opening food stalls at local events.
  • How to join the Chamber of Commerce and Industry and membership fees.
  • How to open a new store in a shopping district.
